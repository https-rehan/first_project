RICE AND BREADS

VEGETABLE BIRYANI ₹280

Fragrant basmati rice cooked with mixed vegetables and aromatic spices, layered to perfection.
Served with a side of cooling raita to balance the rich, layered flavors of the biryani.
Calories: 350 kcal

VEGETABLE PULAO ₹280

A fragrant basmati rice dish cooked with a medley of seasonal vegetables, aromatic spices, and herbs, offering a
delightful and colorful flavor profile. Served with raita or a side of curry.
Calories: 350 kcal

KHICHDI (PLAIN / MASALA) ₹240

A comforting one-pot dish made with rice and lentils, seasoned with plain or masala spices and cooked until soft.
This nutritious and wholesome meal is often served with pickles and yogurt for a satisfying experience.
Calories: 300 kcal

STEAMED RICE ₹170

Fluffy and fragrant basmati rice, perfectly steamed to accompany any dish. A staple that complements curries
and dals beautifully.
Calories: 210 kcal

NAAN (PLAIN / BUTTER / GARLIC / CHEESE) ₹180

Soft and fluffy Indian flatbread baked in a traditional tandoor, available in your choice of plain, buttery, garlic-infused,
or cheesy. Perfect for scooping up curries and dals!
Calories: 250 kcal (Plain), 280 kcal (Butter), 300 kcal (Garlic), 320 kcal (Cheese)

TANDOORI ROTI (PLAIN / BUTTER) ₹160

Whole wheat flatbread baked in a traditional tandoor, available as plain or brushed with butter for added richness.
This roti offers a slightly smoky flavor and a perfect chewy texture, making it an excellent accompaniment to any meal!
Calories: 210 kcal (Plain), 240 kcal (Butter)

PARATHA (PLAIN / BUTTER / ALOO) ₹200

Flaky and golden-brown Indian flatbread, available in your choice of plain, brushed with butter, or stuffed with
spiced potatoes (Aloo). Perfect for pairing with curries or enjoyed on its own!
Calories: 250 kcal (Plain), 280 kcal (Butter), 300 kcal (Aloo)
