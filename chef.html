Meet Our Chef: Chef Aarav Singh


Chef Aarav Singh is the driving force behind Jami-Lo. With over 15 years of experience and a passion for Indian cuisine, he brings a unique touch to every dish.

Signature Dishes

Paneer Butter Masala: Creamy tomato gravy with tender paneer, perfect with naan or rice.

Dal Makhni: Slow-cooked black lentils in a rich, buttery sauce, served with basmati rice.

Mixed Vegetable Curry: A vibrant medley of seasonal vegetables cooked in a fragrant curry sauce, served with roti.


Join us at Jami-Lo Restaurant to experience the culinary artistry of Chef Aarav!

Join us at Jami-Lo Restaurant to experience Chef Anaya's culinary artistry. Her fusion of global tastes and innovative techniques ensures a truly unforgettable dining experience.

Meet Our Chef:Chef Aditya Menon


Chef Aditya Menon is the creative force behind Jami-Lo, with over 14 years of experience in
South Indian and global fusion cuisine. Known for his commitment to authentic vegetarian
flavors and a modern approach, Chef Aditya brings tradition and innovation together in every dish.

Signature Dishes

Mysore Masala Dosa: A crispy dosa filled with spiced mashed potatoes, served with coconut
chutney and tangy sambar, showcasing the beloved flavors of Karnataka.

Malabar Coconut Stew: A rich, coconut-based stew with seasonal vegetables, tempered
with curry leaves and mild spices, served with appam or steamed rice.

Jackfruit Biryani: Aromatic basmati rice layered with tender, spiced jackfruit, topped
with fried onions and fresh herbs—a vegetarian take on a South Indian classic.


Join us at Jami-Lo to experience Chef Aditya’s culinary mastery. His dedication to authentic
South Indian ingredients and inventive techniques promises an unforgettable dining experience.

Meet Our Chef:Chef Vikram Rao


Chef Vikram Rao is the culinary mastermind behind Jami-Lo, bringing over 10 years of experience specializing in Chinese vegetarian cuisine. Meet Our Chef:Chef Raghav Sharma


Chef Raghav Sharma is the culinary expert behind Jami-Lo, bringing over 16 years of
experience specializing in Indian cuisine. With a passion for traditional flavors and a
commitment to quality, Chef Raghav creates dishes that honor the rich heritage of
Indian cooking while incorporating modern techniques and presentations.

Signature Dishes

Chana Masala: A hearty and flavorful dish made with chickpeas simmered in a spiced
tomato and onion gravy, served with warm bhature or rice for a satisfying meal.

Paneer Tikka: Grilled paneer cubes marinated in spiced yogurt, served with a side of
mint chutney and pickled onions, offering a deliciously smoky flavor.

Biryani: Fragrant basmati rice layered with marinated vegetables, aromatic spices,
and saffron, served with raita, showcasing the essence of Indian flavors.


Join us at Jami-Lo to experience Chef Raghav’s culinary artistry. His dedication
to authentic Indian ingredients and innovative techniques promises a truly
unforgettable dining experience that celebrates the richness of Indian cuisine.
